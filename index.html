The Process

  • Source

    We go out of our way to personally pick and evaluate different loose leaf teas from around the world to include in our boxes.

  • Deliver

    At the end of every month we pack a variety of teas, along with the description and brewing instruction of different teas.

  • Enjoy

    You can sit back and enjoy the teas we've curated for you. and it won't be long before next month's box to arrives!

How can Diversitea enrich your tea life?

  • Try teas from around the world

    There are lots of exciting types of tea from around the world. Diversitea can take you on an around-the-world adventure without the expensive plane ticket.
